# Description

A favorite when I go to BBQs (parties, hackathons, your folksâ€™ place), this red berry tart is fairly easy to make and packs a huge wow factor.

# Ingredients

* Graham Cracker Crust
* Vanilla Custard Filling
* Red Berry Dessert Topping

# Steps

1. Bake the crust and let it cool
2. Make the custard, pour into crust
3. Make the red berry topping, spread over the top
